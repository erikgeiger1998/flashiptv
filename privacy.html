Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. It is Flash IPTV's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Flash IPTV.

1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent.

  • Account Data: If you choose to upgrade to Premium, we may collect an email address for account syncing and purchase restoration via RevenueCat.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous diagnostic data (like crash logs) to improve the performance and stability of the app.

2. Content and Playlists

Flash IPTV does NOT provide, host, or track any media content. The app acts strictly as a media player. Any M3U playlists, Xtream Codes credentials, or video streams you input are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your playlists, nor do we track what you watch.

3. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and services to facilitate our app, such as:

  • RevenueCat: Used for managing Premium subscriptions securely.
  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements to non-premium users.

These third parties have access to select diagnostic or purchase data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your watch history, favorites, and profile configurations are stored locally on your device. We don’t share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@flashiptv.com.
